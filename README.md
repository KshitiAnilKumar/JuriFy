# JuriFy

## Decode Law in Seconds, Not Hours

JuriFy is a legal case summarizer built to simplify Indian court judgments.  
Whether you're a law student, advocate, researcher, or just a curious citizen — JuriFy helps you read less and understand more.

---

## 🧠 What Does JuriFy Do?

- Summarizes complex Indian legal judgments into clear, readable briefs.
- Highlights key insights from uploaded court documents.
- Translates summaries into multiple languages.
- Includes a Q&A chatbot that responds only based on the uploaded case.
- Saves chat history and sessions securely.

---

## 👩‍⚖️ Who Is This For?

- Law students preparing for moot courts or internships.
- Legal professionals reviewing large volumes of judgments.
- Paralegals summarizing case files for clients.
- Researchers exploring Indian legal precedents.
- Anyone who wants to understand court decisions without a law degree.
